TraviGuid SAFETY TIPS

To keep you safe, we vet all our chauffeurs and physically inspect all vehicles. Our technology is built with safety in mind but there are also things that you can do to ensure your safety.

  1. Before you make a booking review the safety features in the app so you know how to use them.
  2. Before you get in the car, check that the license plate matchs what's listed in the app.
  3. If you're riding alone, sit in the backseat. This ensures you can safely exit on either side of the vehicle and gives you and your chauffeur some personal space.
  4. Don't share your phone number or other contact information with your chauffeur.
  5. If you ever feel you're in an emergency situation, call 15 immediately.
  6. Tidy up after yourself to keep the car in good condition and ensure the next person has a pleasant ride too.
  7. Never carry firearms in a vehicle.
  8. Rate your journey at the end of the booking. Your feedback helps us improve experience for everyone.
